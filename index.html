About Me

I'm a research-driven professional with almost five years of experience at the intersection of life sciences, AI, and data intelligence. As a qualified botanist with a technical toolkit spanning Python, machine learning, NLP, and metabolomics, I specialise in transforming complex scientific and market data into strategic insights.

I've collaborated with global teams at Enveda Biosciences (Colorado), Royal Botanic Gardens Kew (UK), CURE ID (Tucson), and the Bioinformatics Research Network — building LLM pipelines, metabolomics annotation workflows, knowledge graphs, and drug discovery intelligence systems.

I'm passionate about bridging traditional botanical knowledge with modern computational methods, particularly in how AI can accelerate drug development and health research.
